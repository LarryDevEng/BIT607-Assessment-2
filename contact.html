Frequently Asked Questions:

How long does a typical grooming session take?

A basic bath and brush takes 1-2 hours, while a full grooming package takes 2-3 hours. We'll provide a more accurate estimate when you book based on your dog's specific needs.

Do you offer special services for puppies or senior dogs?

Yes! We have puppy packages ($30-$60) and senior dog packages ($40-$80). Both include gentle handling and care tailored to your dog's age and needs.

What's included in your Full Grooming Package?

Our Full Grooming Package ($60-$120) includes:

  • Bath with premium shampoo
  • Professional haircut
  • Nail trim and ear cleaning
  • Teeth brushing

How often should I get my dog groomed?

Grooming frequency varies by breed and coat type:

  • Short-haired breeds: Every 6-8 weeks
  • Long-haired breeds: Every 4-6 weeks
  • Continuously growing hair: Every 3-4 weeks

Do you offer any special treatments?

Yes, we offer medicated baths ($20-$40 additional), de-shedding treatments ($20-$40), and flea treatments ($15-$30). Please mention any special requirements when booking.
