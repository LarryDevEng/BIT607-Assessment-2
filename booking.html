Booking form

Book Your Dog's Grooming Appointment

Booking form instructions:

  • All fields are required - you won't be able to submit the form without completing them
  • Owner's Name:
    • Must be between 5-50 characters long
    • Please enter your full name as it appears on your ID
  • Phone:
    • Must be between 5-25 characters long
  • Email:
    • Must be a valid email format (example@domain.com)
    • This will be used for booking confirmation and updates
  • Dog's Name:
    • Must be between 5-50 characters long
    • Please use the name your dog responds to
    • This helps us communicate better with your pet during grooming
  • Dog's Breed:
    • Must be between 5-50 characters long
    • Please be as specific as possible (e.g., "Miniature Poodle" rather than just "Poodle")
  • Dog's Age:
    • Must be between 0-30 years
    • For puppies under 1 year, please enter 0
  • Additional Notes:
    • After submission, you will receive a confirmation email
    • Please allow up to 24 hours for us to process your booking request
    • If you need to make changes after submitting, please contact us directly
    • For emergency bookings, please call us instead of using the online form
    • We may need to contact you for additional information about your dog's specific grooming needs
